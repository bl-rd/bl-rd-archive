This website

I was inspired to put this website together after coming across a handful of awesome personal websites (notably this blog article by Jamie Tanner) and the indieweb movement.

This inspiration came around the same time I was looking to get back into web design, and felt like it would be a good opportunity to do just that.

I also wanted to challenge myself as in the past I've always been reluctant (read: terrified) of putting stuff on social media. As others have pointed out, a personal site feels like a safe way to put stuff out there. Most likely it will just disappear into the void, but if people find it and it read it then, well, that's fine!

This site is powered by eleventy and netlify (the cool kids' combo of the month). The source code is available to view on GitHub.
