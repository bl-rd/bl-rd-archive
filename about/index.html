Work

I currently work at Lancaster University, one of the top 10 universities in the UK.

I am a developer in a small team, focusing on providing platforms such as the Student Portal and Staff Intranet, as well as APIs and systems for developers and staff at the University.

Technically, I guess I'm a full-stack developer, using .NET and SQL Server on the back-end, but really I consider myself a front-end developer.

If I had to commit, my JavaScript framework of choice is currently Vue, but where possible I prefer to write well-structured semantic HTML, with plain old CSS and JavaScript. I put a fair few (but not all!) of my side projects on GitHub, feel free to have a look.

My favourite part of my job is making performant, accessible and progressively enhanced websites.

Feel free to have a look at my full CV on LinkedIn.

Not work

I’m a dad so I don’t actually have that much free time. When I can make the time, I enjoy climbing and skateboarding (knees allowing).

Going full in on better late than never, I’m also tentatively dipping my toes into the world of making things that don’t involve code.
