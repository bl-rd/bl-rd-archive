I'm a human who makes web things

Work

I currently work at Lancaster University, one of the top 10 universities in the UK.

I am a developer in a small team, focusing on providing platforms such as the Student Portal and Staff Intranet, as well as APIs and systems for developers and staff at the University.

Technically, I guess I'm a full-stack developer, using .NET and SQL Server on the back-end, but really I consider my self a front-end developer! My JavaScript framework of choice is currently Vue, but where possible I prefer to write well-structured semantic HTML, with plain old CSS and JavaScript. Feel free to have a look at some of my side projects on GitHub1.

My favourite part of my job is making performant, accessible and progressively enhanced websites.

Not work

I’m a dad so I don’t actually have that much free time. When I can make the time, I enjoy climbing and skateboarding (when my knees allow it!).

Going full in on better late than never, I’m also tentatively dipping my toes into the world of making things that don’t involve code.
