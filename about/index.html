Work

I currently work at Lancaster University, one of the top 10 universities in the UK.

If I had to commit, my JavaScript framework of choice is currently Vue, but where possible I prefer to write well-structured semantic HTML, with plain old CSS and JavaScript. I put a fair few (but not all!) of my side projects on GitHub, feel free to have a look.

My favourite part of my job is making performant, accessible and progressively enhanced websites.
